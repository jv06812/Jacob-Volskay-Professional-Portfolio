About Me

I am an advanced manufacturing professional specializing in aerospace CNC programming, process optimization, automation architecture, and production system development within AS9100/ITAR-regulated environments, and am a mechanical engineering student.

Operating at the intersection of hands-on production and engineering-level analysis, I own the full lifecycle of manufacturing initiatives — from process planning and Fusion 360 CAD/CAM programming, to post processor modification, machine troubleshooting, automation optimization and reliability, fixturing and workholding design, and ROI modeling.

My work spans multi-axis platforms including CNC 3-axis, 4-axis, 5-axis and turning centers, with experience machining titanium, Inconel, PH stainless, and aluminum components for aerospace power generation, automotive and agricultural industries.
